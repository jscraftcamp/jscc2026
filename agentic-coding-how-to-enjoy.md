# Agentic coding - how to enjoy?

hosted by @matthias-stemmler

Comments by some of the participants:

- Sucks the joy out of software development
- Traditionally it's a craft
- No way around it anymore
- Feel like losing control over my project
- Learning is the fun
- Who has fun vibe-coding? -> audience is divided 50/50
- AI is the fastest way to learn anything
- You think you're learning a lot, but actually not so much
- "Change your attitude"
- You'll learn different things
- Team aspect: We're not talking that much anymore
- Context switches are very exhausting
- Run multiple agents in parallel, but don't go round-robin answering questions, but focus just on writing specifications
- Sweet spot was tab-completion e.g. with Cursor
- Waiting is what kills the joy
