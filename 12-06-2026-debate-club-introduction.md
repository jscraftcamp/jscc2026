**‚Sexier‘**

**S** satement (what‘s my argument)

**Ex** Explantation of the statement (how/why is that)

**I** Illustration (anecdote - *nice to have*)

**E** Explanation of the Illustration (illustration might be weird, so it needs context why)

**R** Relevant (why is this relevant - how does this tie in the debate and how does this cement my position)

*If it‘s possible to aks why after your statement, then you have to explain further. Maybe it‘s not obvious why the thing that you claimed is bad is actually bad.*
